# HealthScope AI

## Project Overview

HealthScope AI is a healthcare intelligence platform designed to aggregate public health data, calculate healthcare risk indicators, and generate analytical insights through interactive dashboards.

The platform combines data from multiple public healthcare and demographic sources to support comparative analysis and informed decision making.

---

## Problem Statement

Healthcare information is often distributed across multiple sources and presented in formats that are difficult to analyze.

Decision makers, researchers, and analysts require a centralized platform capable of transforming raw health statistics into actionable insights.

---

## Project Goals

- Aggregate healthcare datasets from public APIs
- Compute healthcare risk indicators
- Visualize health trends
- Compare countries and regions
- Generate automated analytical insights
- Provide exportable reports

---

## Success Criteria

Users can:

- Search countries
- View health metrics
- Compare countries
- Generate health insights
- Export reports

---

## Target Audience

- Healthcare Analysts
- Researchers
- Students
- Public Health Professionals
- Policy Makers
